About GPA

We are FUTA's premier tutorial community dedicated to helping students achieve academic excellence through personalized learning approaches.

Who We Are

We know what it feels like to hit a wall—when a concept just won't click, or a test date looms large. At Genius Point Academy, we built a community, not just a tutoring service, dedicated to helping students move past frustration and into genuine mastery and confidence.

Our mission is simple: To provide the precise support you need, when you need it, ensuring no student is left behind.

Our Mission

To empower students with personalized learning strategies that transform academic challenges into opportunities for growth and success.

Our Vision

To become the leading academic support community where every student discovers their potential and achieves breakthrough results.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tutoring services, pricing, and booking process.

1. Is tutor booking free?

No, tutor booking is not free but it's highly affordable. Personal tutorials start at ₦1,500, group tutorials are ₦500-₦1,000 per student, and mass tutorials are ₦5,000 per 2hrs.

2. How does the pricing work?

Personal: ₦1,500-₦2,000/session | Group: ₦500-₦1,000/student | Mass: ₦5,000 per 2hrs. Free introductory classes also available.

3. How long is each tutoring session?

Standard sessions are 2 hours long. Extended sessions (3-4 hours) available at additional cost.

4. Where do tutoring sessions take place?

On campus at FUTA, online via Zoom/Google Meet/WhatsApp, or hybrid. You specify your preference when booking.

5. How do I pay for tutoring sessions?

Bank transfer, mobile money (Opay/Palmpay/Moniepoint), or cash on arrangement. Payment before each session.

6. What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

24+ hours notice: full refund/reschedule. 12-24 hours: 50% refund. Less than 12 hours: reschedule with fee. Emergencies handled case-by-case.

7. How are tutors selected and verified?

Rigorous selection: academic verification, teaching ability assessment, subject expertise testing, reference checks, trial sessions, and continuous monitoring.

8. Can I request a specific tutor?

Yes! Request specific tutors during booking. Availability depends on their schedule. Alternatives suggested if unavailable.

9. What materials are provided?

Customized study notes, practice problems, past questions, concept maps, digital resource library access, and progress tracking sheets.

10. How do I book a tutor?

Fill the booking form, select tutorial type, choose course/tutor, submit. We contact you within 24hrs to confirm. Pay and start learning!
