Who We Are

We know what it feels like to hit a wall—when a concept just won't click, or a test date looms large. At Genius Point Academy, we built a community, not just a tutoring service, dedicated to helping students move past frustration and into genuine mastery and confidence.

Our mission is simple: To provide the precise support you need, when you need it, ensuring no student is left behind.

Our Vision

Your Path to Clarity

We understand that learning isn't one-size-fits-all, so we offer flexible, high-impact environments designed around you:

1:1 Personalized Tutorials

This is where the magic happens. You work side-by-side with a dedicated mentor who digs into your specific struggles, custom-builds a strategy just for you, and focuses on true understanding—not just memorization.

Private Group Tutoring

Bring your study group, share the cost, and thrive together! We offer focused, collaborative sessions where you can debate concepts, solve problems as a team, and reinforce learning under the guidance of an expert.

Mass Tutorials

Need a quick, powerful refresh before a major exam? Join our engaging, large-scale sessions designed to efficiently demystify entire topics, consolidate knowledge, and boost your curriculum confidence quickly.

Real Education, Real Access

We believe expertise should be accessible to everyone. That's why we offer a blend of support to get you started and keep you going:

Free Classes

Jump in immediately! Access essential introductory workshops, foundational subject reviews, and large-scale concept previews to build momentum and get a feel for our community—no commitment necessary.

Paid Classes

Unlock premium resources, intensive exam prep bootcamps, and the dedicated time of our expert tutors through our specialized group and personalized programs.
