Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tutoring services, pricing, and booking process.

1. Is tutor booking free?

No, tutor booking is not free but it's highly affordable. We offer quality tutoring services at reasonable prices: Personal tutorials start at ₦1,500, group tutorials are ₦500-₦1,000 per student (based on group size), and mass tutorial pricing is available upon request. We believe in fair compensation for our tutors' expertise while keeping education accessible.

2. How does the pricing work?

Our pricing structure is simple and transparent:

  • Personal Tutorials: ₦1,500 - ₦2,000 per session (2 hours), depending on tutor experience and course complexity
  • Group Tutorials: ₦1,000/student for 5-10 students | ₦500/student for 10+ students
  • Mass Tutorials: ₦5,000 per 2-hour session (for 20+ students)
  • Free Classes: We offer free introductory workshops and concept previews
3. How long is each tutoring session?

Standard tutoring sessions are 2 hours long. This gives enough time for concept explanation, problem-solving practice, and Q&A. Extended sessions (3-4 hours) are available at additional cost, especially useful for exam preparation or intensive topic coverage.

4. Where do tutoring sessions take place?

Sessions can be conducted:

  • On Campus: Designated study areas at FUTA
  • Online: Via Zoom, Google Meet, or WhatsApp video
  • Hybrid: Combination of in-person and online sessions
  • At Designated Locations: Pre-arranged convenient locations

You can specify your preference when booking.

5. How do I pay for tutoring sessions?

We offer multiple payment options for your convenience:

  • Bank Transfer: Direct bank deposit
  • Mobile Money: Opay, Palmpay, Moniepoint
  • Cash Payment: On arrangement
  • Online Payment: Available upon request

Payment is typically made before or at the beginning of each session. For multiple sessions, you can pay upfront for a discount.

6. What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

We understand that schedules can change. Our cancellation/rescheduling policy:

  • 24+ hours notice: Full refund or reschedule at no cost
  • 12-24 hours notice: 50% refund or reschedule with small fee
  • Less than 12 hours: No refund but can reschedule with fee
  • Emergency situations: Case-by-case consideration
7. How are tutors selected and verified?

All GPA tutors undergo a rigorous selection process:

  • Academic verification (current FUTA students with proven records)
  • Teaching ability assessment
  • Subject matter expertise testing
  • Reference checks
  • Trial teaching sessions
  • Continuous performance monitoring
8. Can I request a specific tutor?

Yes! You can request specific tutors during the booking process. However, tutor availability depends on their schedule. If your preferred tutor is unavailable, we'll suggest similar qualified tutors or help you find another suitable time.

9. What materials are provided?

Depending on the tutorial type, we provide:

  • Customized study notes and summaries
  • Practice problems with solutions
  • Past question papers and solutions
  • Concept maps and study guides
  • Access to our digital resource library
  • Progress tracking sheets
10. How do I book a tutor?

Booking is simple:

  1. Fill out the booking form on our website
  2. Select your preferred tutorial type (personal/group/mass)
  3. Choose your course and preferred tutor
  4. Select your preferred contact method
  5. Submit the form
  6. We'll contact you within 24 hours to confirm details
  7. Make payment and start learning!

You can also message us directly on WhatsApp for immediate assistance.
